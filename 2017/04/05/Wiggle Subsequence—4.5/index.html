Wiggle Subsequence

发表于 2017-04-05 | 分类于 算法设计 |

The Question

A sequence of numbers is called a wiggle sequence if the differences between successive numbers strictly alternate between positive and negative.

The first difference (if one exists) may be either positive or negative. A sequence with fewer than two elements is trivially a wiggle sequence.

For example, [1,7,4,9,2,5] is a wiggle sequence because the differences (6,-3,5,-7,3) are alternately positive and negative. In contrast, [1,4,7,2,5] and [1,7,4,5,5] are not wiggle sequences, the first because its first two differences are positive and the second because its last difference is zero.

Given a sequence of integers, return the length of the longest subsequence that is a wiggle sequence. A subsequence is obtained by deleting some number of elements (eventually, also zero) from the original sequence, leaving the remaining elements in their original order.

The Example

  • Input: [1,7,4,9,2,5] Output: 6
    The entire sequence is a wiggle sequence.
  • Input: [1,17,5,10,13,15,10,5,16,8] Output: 7
    There are several subsequences that achieve this length. One is [1,17,10,13,10,16,8].
  • Input: [1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9] Output: 2

一组数组，求其最大摆动子序列，注意这里的子序列不一定指的是连续子序列。所谓的摆动子序列就是序列中的数据增长趋势是递增和递减交替进行，数据的曲线表示就是来回的折线

The Anwser

解这个问题的核心就是记录数据的增长的变化情况是递增还是递减，或者是不变的数据。

  1. 使用一个int变量，其中1代表递增，0代表递减，2代表数据不变（和前一个数据一样大小）；
  2. 查看数组的长度，当长度小于等于1的时候，总结返回数组的大小；
  3. 当数组大于等于2的时候，首先摆动子序列的最小长度就位1；
  4. 开始比较时，当当前的数组的是递增趋势的话，那么在其之前的数组应该是递减或者保持不变的趋势，摆动子序列才进行更新；
  5. 当当前的数组是递减趋势的话，那么在其之前的数组应该是递增或者保持不变的趋势，摆动子序列才进行更新。
